About Me

Hi! I recently completed my Ph.D. in Linguistics at the University of Oregon, where I worked with Kris Kyle in the Learner Corpus Research and Applied Data Science (LCR-ADS) Lab. I’ve been especially interested in developing and validating lexicogrammatical indices to empirically evaluate second language learners’ productive proficiency. Outside of academia, I’m passionate about long-distance running.

In Fall 2025, I will join the Rochester Institute of Technology (Department of Psychology) as an Assistant Professor.
